MIR AMERICAS 2021 The 100% carbon fiber racing helmet used by champion Joan Mir at the 2021 Grand Prix of the Americas, dedicated to legend Kevin Schwantz.
The PISTA GP RR LIMITED EDITION MIR AMERICAS 2021 celebrates the meeting of two Suzuki icons. Joan Mir, 2020 MotoGP™ World Champion, pays tribute to the legendary Kevin Schwantz, who won the 1993 MotoGP™ World Championship with the Hamamatsu-based manufacturer, on the helmet worn by the Spanish rider at the 2021 Grand Prix of the Americas in Austin, Texas. Pista GP RR is an exact replica of the AGV helmet used by champions in the World Championship. It has therefore received the new FIM homologation , which certifies the highest possible level of protection, even against any dangerous twisting of the head.
